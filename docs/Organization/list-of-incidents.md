# List of Incidents

After you login to TheHive application, you can see a list of incidents displayed on the screen by default. 

<img src="../images/list_of_incidents.png" alt="list of incidents" width="1000" height="1000"/>

## Main Menu
On the Left hand side the user can see the main Menu. 

 - Case List
 - Alerts
 - Tasks
 - Dashboards
 - Search 
 - Organization

 Clicking on the arrow will switch the user back to the image view. 

 <img src="../images/main_menu.png" alt="menu" width="200" height="100"/>

## Top Menu

On the top of the page are the following options: 

- Defaults
- Quick Filters
- Auto Refresh
- Stats
- Filter

### Defaults

It displays the default view. 

1. Click on **Default**. A list is displayed. 

<img src="../images/default_view.png" alt="default view" width="300" height="300"/>

To Save a view: 

1. Click **Save view As**

A new window opens.

<img src="../images/save_current_view.png" alt="save current view" width="300" height="300"/>

To Manage views: 

1. Click on the default button. 
1. Click on the **Manage Views** from the list. 

<img src="../images/manage_view_option.png" alt="manage custom tags" width="300" height="300"/>

A new page opens. It has the **Name of the view** and the corresponding **Actions**. 

1. Click the **...**. 
1. Select the action. e.g. Delete

<img src="../images/manage_views.png" alt="manage views" width="1000" height="1000"/>

### Quick Filters

To apply Quick filter:

1. Click the **quick filter** option. 
2. The list displays options to select from. 

<img src="../images/quick-filters.png" alt="quick filters" width="300" height="300"/>

### Auto refresh
The auto-refresh option allows the user to automatically refresh a page. 

To perform Auto refresh:

1. On the page, in the tab, switch on the Auto refresh button. 

<img src="../images/autorefresh.png" alt="auto refresh" width="1000" height="1000"/>


### Stats

To view Stats: 

1. On the cases list page, switch on the Stats toggle button, the stats will be displayed. 

<img src="../images/Stats.png" alt="stats" width="1000" height="1000"/>


### Filters

To apply filter:

1. On the cases list page, switch on the Filters toggle button.

1. Click **Add filters**.

Apply Filter to the required field.

<img src="../images/apply_filter.png" alt="apply filters" width="1000" height="1000"/>

 1. Select the filters from the list.
 1. Click Apply filters.
 1. (Optional) Click Clear filters to clear all applied filters.
