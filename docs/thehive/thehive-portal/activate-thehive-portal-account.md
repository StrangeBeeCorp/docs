# Activate Your TheHive Portal Account

The Security team has invited you to access specific security cases through TheHive Portal because your involvement or awareness is needed for incidents affecting your area of responsibility.

<h2>Procedure</h2>

1. Open your TheHive Portal invitation email and click the activation link.

    !!! note "Activation link expiration"
        The activation link expires after a limited time. If your link has expired, contact your administrator for a new invitation.

2. On the **Reset your password** page, enter your email address and select **Send**.

    ![Reset password](/thehive/images/user-guides/reset-password.png)

    You will receive another email with a link to replace the system-generated default password with your own secure password. The link is valid for a limited time.

3. Open the email and click the link. On the **Set a new password** page, enter your new password, then select **Set password**.

    ![Set new password](/thehive/images/user-guides/set-new-password.png)

    You will receive a confirmation email.

4. Log in with your email address and new password.

5. Review the **Cases** view to see any cases already shared with you.

    ![Case list TheHive Portal](/thehive/images/user-guides/thehive-portal/case-list-thehive-portal.png)

6. Navigate through cases using the available tools:

    * Review the [case glossary](glossary-thehive-portal.md) to understand case terminology and elements
    * Use [filters and sorting](find-a-case-thehive-portal.md#method-2-filters-in-the-cases-view) or the [search bar](find-a-case-thehive-portal.md#method-1-search-bar) to narrow down your case list
    * Use the [search bar](find-a-case-thehive-portal.md#method-1-search-bar) to find specific cases

7. Your available actions depend on your role and the reason you have access to TheHive Portal.

    You may be able to:

    * [View the security issues shared with you](find-a-case-thehive-portal.md)
    * [Post a comment](add-comment-cases-shared-thehive-portal.md)
    * [Upload a document](add-attachment-cases-shared-thehive-portal.md)
    * [Share relevant details](add-observable-cases-shared-thehive-portal.md)
    * [Report a new security issue](create-case-thehive-portal.md)
